## Product Description

**RAK4200 WisDuo LPWAN Module** includes an STM32L071 MCU and an SX1276 LoRa chip. It has Ultra-Low Power Consumption of 9.40&nbsp;uA (down to 1.08&nbsp;Î¼A @ 2.0&nbsp;V) in sleep mode and high LoRa output power up to 19&nbsp;dBm max in work mode.
