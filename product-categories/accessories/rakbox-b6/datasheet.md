# WisBlock RAKBox-B6 Enclosure Datasheet

RAKBox-B6 is an IP20-rated enclosure for indoor usage. It is designed specifically for WisBlock products.

## Lightweight Plastic Design

- The enclosure is compact and lightweight, ideal for space-constrained setups.
- The slots in the cover allow airflow through the enclosure for applications that measure ambient environmental parameters like temperature, humidity, gases, and air quality.
- Supports easy wall-mounting with screws.
- Easy access to the USB port allows recharging and reprogramming of the internal WisBlock without opening the enclosure.
- Lid to close USB access

## Mechanical Characteristics

- Enclosure material: ABS UL94-HB
- Dimensions: 84.5 mm x 84.5 mm x 36.7 mm (W x L x H)
- Weight: approximately 56.6 g (without mounting plate)
- Material thickness: 2.15 mm
- Supports wall mounting
- Ingress Protection: IP20
