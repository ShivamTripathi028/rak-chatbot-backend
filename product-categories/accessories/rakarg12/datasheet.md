# 860-930 MHz 3dBi Fiberglass Antenna Datasheet

## Overview

### Features

- Frequency Range: 860 ~ 930 MHz
- One antenna to suit both 868 MHz and 915 MHz bands
- Max Gain: 3 dBi
- High efficiency
- Vertically polarized monopole
- Omnidirectional
- Outdoor use
