Battery Plus is the RAK's latest battery system incorporating a solar charging system and status monitoring. It is specially developed for the WisGate Edge Pro gateways. It will allow reliable outdoor deployment of the gateway in remote off-grid scenarios where the power is unreliable or inaccessible.
